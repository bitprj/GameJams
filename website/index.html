About Gameathon

Hosted by

Gameathon is a 2 week event hosted by Bit Project in collaboration with industry partners, where anyone can learn the skills needed to make your own video game! Spend a week familiarizing yourself with some of the most popular game development engines used in the industry today, and then spend a week coding the best game you can. No prior experience is necessary, and everyone is welcome to join. Submit a game, and you'll be in the running for hundreds of dollars in prizes!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Game Jam, exactly?

A game jam is like a hackathon, but specifically for games! Essentially, you’ll have a week to program and submit the best game you can, and we’ll judge the games and pick our favorites.

Who can participate - how much experience do I need?

Anyone with an interest in programming or game development can participate. As a matter of fact, we encourage people with 0 prior experience with game development to join!

Are we allowed to form teams?

This is an individual event, so no teams. However, you’re encouraged to get your friends to join and troubleshoot together! We'll also be hosting social events like stand-ups throughout the development week to promote socializing and friendly collaboration.

How can I interact with other participants -- is there any virtual platform where we can all communicate?

As a matter of fact, there is! We have an official Bit Project Discord, which everyone is encouraged to join. Also, we'll be periodically hosting events that encourage socializing between participants. Keep your eyes peeled for those!

What kind of workshops are being held?

We’ll be hosting workshops to teach someone with no prior knowledge how to use both the GameMaker and Unity engines. In addition, we’ll be having guest speakers discuss more general game development concepts.

How do we submit our games?

As we approach the start of the game jam, we’ll elaborate on how the games are meant to be submitted.
